About me

My Projects

Apartments App

Apartments App is a streamlined approach aiming to reduce hassle and increase accessibility for both renters and landlords, making the rental process more efficient overall.

Viora

Viora is a website that allows users to browse questions and answers to certain topics. In a constantly updating feed, users can see posts relevant to them or the topic they are viewing. Each topic has posts in a "did you know?" format, with an adjoining section allowing users to ask actual question about a topic.

Ingestibles

Ingestibles is a DIY cookbook website that allows users to check out, or post their own recipes. Recipes are categorized and easy to find, especially with the ability to search for the recipe that you like. Simply begin to type in the search bar, and a list of smart results will display below.

Groovium

Groovium is a 1970s-themed blog post website. Users can write stories of their own, or they can read articles written by others. Users can easily find authors that they like with the click of the follow button, or they can reference back to stories they have bookmarked for future reading.

Eventually

Eventually is a sale event website. Users can find sale events to attend, such as yard sales, garage sales, estate sales, etc. Users can also create sale events of their own. An address input is required, this address will place your sale on a Google Map for other users to find.
